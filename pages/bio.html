Bio

Vill is a DJ and Producer with a name in Lisbon, currently residing at Rive Rouge. He performed for K Urban Beach and has also performed several times at Nox, in Lagos. In addition, he also performed in a beach party for 500 people in Salou, Spain.

His performance style ranges from a sunset party vibe, with deep-house music, to the big nightclubs with house, nu-disco, bass-house, techno etc. Vill has the ability to bring all the sub-genres of House Music together with the great hits that take place at the time.

In his music production, he has presented many projects with a more Chillout / Deep-House vibe and has been an increasingly surprising revelation with his projects.

Vill has grown a lot as a national artist and is already planning going international.
